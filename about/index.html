About us

We are providing a simple tool to track your driving and filing your expenses. It can be done in many ways – but we are here to create the most simple – painless solution on the market. In everything we do we try and make the scenario for the user as easy as possible but at the same time give them an eye pleasing experience so they actually want to use our tool and not only the result that it provides them.

Record a new trip

Press record and let 80 track how far you drive. When you arrive, you can save your trip with a little note and your trip is securely stored on your iPhone.

Review your trips

View your recent trips by swiping back and forth on the Dashboard, or open the full history to see all of your past trips.

Export your trips

Whenever you need to reimburse your mileage, you can export your trips by selecting a date range and send the list by e-mail, save to Dropbox, open in Numbers, Excel, Google Sheets or any other CSV-compatible app.
